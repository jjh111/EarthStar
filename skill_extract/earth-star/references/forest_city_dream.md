## The Complete Dream Narrative

### Arrival

I find myself on a large balcony overlooking this amazing city of megatrees, large as apartment complexes. Such lush abundance everywhere. People are hovering about in the sky! There are people on the balcony‚Äîadults and children of all ages. Smiling so wide at me. And I knew: they were my descendants!

Then kids were all over me, asking a billion questions:
- "What was it like when we kicked the ass of the old oligarchs?"
- "When did violence against others first end?"
- "What was your scariest mission?"
- "Did you really do that famous thing?"

My head is swirling under the barrage of happy yet relentless child curiosity. They are expecting me to answer, but I don't know what to say.

"To be honest," I stammer out, "I don't think any of this has like 'happened' yet from my perspective. I don't know what you kids are asking about!"

The adults do an awkward look around moment, realizing that they were also being fans without checking to make sure I was properly calibrated to this huge shift in timeline!

### The Recognition

"We asked the treenet to bring you here," one of them told me, "I suppose assuming you'd be the elder one. Earth must have known better to bring you here. We are in awe of you, for what you did in your lifetime worked! You and the others enabled this, planted this world we enjoy."

I look around again. It's just breathtaking‚Äîthere's no way to describe. The air seems to hum with vitality. Everyone looks so healthy and happy.

### The Armor

"This might not make sense to you then," one of the younger adults who was hanging back comes forward, "but we fabbed your armor, the famous one you and the Stewards used to end devastation."

He shows me these incredible wireframe shoulder guards. It looks gossamer thin, yet each structural element has so many different glints coming off it, it's mesmerizing. There's a subtle vibration to what looks like diamond structure of it.

"Well," he continues, "We didn't do it exactly to spec from your era, we took some liberties that we hoped you'd enjoy." And he looks around to some other folks now gathered as he was telling me this that looked really interested in my reaction.

I was reeling for real now! Because I was just this John typing to you now, living in 2025, and I have no idea what he's talking about. Like I know I write this blog and daydream, but these folks were so relaxed in their certainty that I was this historic figure that did what now?

It's odd because the whole time in this dream I'm reeling, but recognizing it all. The details of the world are so real, it has a delightful air quality, but I'm not catching names.

This armor now in front of me just looks so perfect and solid, and yet it's like liquid diamond. I can tell that it would be able to rip a tank in half, survive any projectile as I can see it subtly vibrating. Glints and glimmers of reflected light seem to tease my eyes, as if saying 'you can only see this because it's letting you'. And I somehow knew this one was built for peace, and now I wonder what the one built for cleanup will look like‚Ä¶

### The Flight

One of the older adults comes in seemingly clocking what is happening to my little brain. "I'm sure he's super excited about this whole visit, so maybe let's do a demonstration." They're pointing to the armor resting on a pillow on the ground.

"Ya I can use this?" I ask, delighted. Flying with jetpacks in my dreams is a favorite pastime of mine. So this reconnects me to the forward motion of this dream.

"Of course," they say, "We are hoping to give you a tour of this megatree city before you have to go back to your time."

The shoulder guard seems to jump up on me, fits perfectly as what I thought was rigid diamond seems to deform and conform to my shoulders. Then the sweet thruster wings pop out!

I boost off and the younglings follow. (Ha, younglings! I'm 34 now but to these people I'm like 1000 years their elder it feels like.)

Gliding around, I'm getting the feel for this jetpack. Looking down I can see the tops of the megatrees, the different crop areas, the footpaths for synthriders and striders. It's so tangible. I fly closer to a megatree, see the bark and wave to folks in their balconies, grown right into the tree by the tree. I smell grilled food from the street level, but I'm having too much fun flying so I rise higher to go faster out of the literal forest of the city's occupants.

### The Kids' Flight Technology

"That armor's pretty fast!" one kid, seemingly without any method, is flying right next to me. I decide to test a full stop and start hovering over what looks like a central area of the city tree village. The river glistens and I can see elegant patterns of tapering canals watering the city passively yet comprehensively. Like old times made new.

"How are you guys flying?" I ask. I can kind of see or feel like a vibration coming off him and the other few kids who are hovering with me.

"We're like giant bumblebees, just amped up immensely. We use the resonant frequency given off by the earth's Schumann resonance coupled with the tree city itself. It's like way more cool than lift and thrust cause you can do this‚Äî" One kid blurts out as he moves a body-length up, down, left and right with perfect precision right angles.

"It's only possible cause of the megatrees so thanks." Another kid says and shies away a little.

"Wow that's amazing!" I just love this all so much I'm overcome with a strange kind of mega grandpa energy that I don't know how to describe. "You kids fly all the time?"

They look at each other confused. "Uh yeah everyone gets everywhere like this at least a few times a day. Some of us race for fun."

### The Play

Hovering here in this forest world, so unlike what I live in as an earthling today in 2025, and yet so familiar. All of it grown from what I am living in 2025, this end of days we are living in together. My imagination grown up to surprise me with a vivid dream of flying with my future kin.

They who are levitating upon the social fabric of their wonderful world made real by the bioelectromagnetic power of Gaiarchitecture. Living technology, resonance, everything I'm striving for in this life right now as I type this.

"Are you ok?" One asks, because I think I'm crying.

"Hey!" I deflect and decide to have some fun because I can tell my time in this dream future is limited. "I'm gonna show you whippersnappers what lift and thrust can really do. Try and catch me if you can!"

I give it full power and my body rockets forward. I push through their little group hover sending them tumbling. How fast can this thing go? A kind of field is around my head so it's always comfortable as the world starts blurring. The kids are right on my tail. I try a hard left and dip down. I'm finding some serious G's in these turns. They just angle right over, not bothering with my vector at all.

This is not like dogfighting with planes from my childhood videogame experience!

I make a lazy arc back up toward them, and just as they are all reaching up for me cutting the arc I stop thrusting and they run into each other as I fall backwards away laughing.

We go on like this for a while, the ion thruster rocket things or whatever this is I'm wearing absolutely humming with power.

Eventually they get me because their resonant way of hovering to fly really is astounding. I was working so hard while they could just pick a point in the sky they wanted it seemed like!

### The Return

We fly back to the balcony where this wonderful dream began, to find the adults waiting. The kids got bored and went off to play. Classic.

I land and a woman in flowing linen comes forward to meet me. She is wearing a delicate tiara made of one piece of diamond with gold insets.

"Thank you so much for coming, this means so much to us to make the history real for the children."

I just have to laugh. "Thank you for giving me this gift, it would have been enough to stand on this balcony overlooking this world. And you all made me a jetpack?" I'm truly touched.

I have this feeling, I voice as, "I don't know when I can come back here..."

She looks at me with caring eyes, a hint of mischief in her smile. "I suppose we shall see. But now we want you to know that we do make it. We exist, a world better than yours thanks to what you did. You solve the puzzle of the 21st century. So we'll be waiting for you here in your legacy."

### Awakening

And I woke up in my bed here in 2025. The vision was so clear, the comfort lingered. Whether real or not, I now carry this kind of strange certainty in what we do. I felt it and still do: **we make it.**

The doom does not prevail. We are the stewards of a better world. In this era of the 21st century we can say 'Never again' to tyranny and waste. Our actions will become a forest world for all our future descendants.
