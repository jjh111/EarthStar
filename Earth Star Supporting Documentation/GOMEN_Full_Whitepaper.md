# 1. INTRODUCTION & CURRENT TECHNOLOGY LIMITATIONS

Current electronics depend on 17 rare earth elements extracted primarily from environmentally devastating mining operations. The human and ecological costs are staggering:

- **Energy cost**: 50-200 MWh per ton of refined material
- **Water consumption**: 1000-5000 L per kg

- **Human cost**: Documented slave labor, child mining, community displacement
- **Geopolitical risk**: 85% supply from China, creating vulnerability
- **Finite reserves**: 30-100 year horizon for key elements

- **Carbon nanotubes**: 100x strength of steel at 1/6 weight
